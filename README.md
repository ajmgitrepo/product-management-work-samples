# Sanitized Product Management Work Samples Portfolio

A curated portfolio of product management artifacts demonstrating strategy, discovery, execution, technical product thinking, stakeholder alignment, and measurable outcomes across cloud infrastructure, internal developer platforms, Kubernetes/CaaS, LBaaS, cloud migration, and AI-assisted workflows.

> Note: All materials are sanitized, generalized, and written as representative work samples. No confidential employer, customer, or proprietary data is included.

## Portfolio Positioning

This repo is designed for senior / principal-level Product Manager, Technical Product Manager, Platform PM, and Product Lead opportunities. It shows the ability to move from ambiguity to strategy, translate technical complexity into business outcomes, drive cross-functional execution, and measure product impact.
